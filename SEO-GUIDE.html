🔍 Diamond Solar — Google Ranking (SEO) Guide

Website me technical SEO already daal diya gaya hai (meta tags, schema, sitemap, robots.txt). Ab Google me top par aane ke liye ye steps follow karo.

⚠️ Sabse pehle: SEO tabhi sahi kaam karta hai jab apna domain ho (jaise diamondsolar.in). Abhi GitHub ka address hai. Domain lene ke baad neeche wale steps karo.

STEP 0 — Domain lene ke baad (developer ka kaam)

Website ke code me filhaal placeholder domain www.diamondsolar.in daala hua hai. Jab actual domain final ho jaye to saari files me ye address apne real domain se replace karna hoga (find & replace). Ye ek baar ka kaam hai.

STEP 1 — Google Search Console (Free)

  1. search.google.com/search-console par jao, Gmail se login karo.
  2. "Add Property" → apna domain daalo.
  3. Ownership verify karo (hosting ke DNS me ek record add karna hoga — Console guide dega ya hosting support se karwa lo).
  4. Verify hone ke baad: left menu → Sitemaps → ye daalo: sitemap.xml → Submit.
✅ Isse Google ko saari pages ek saath pata chal jayengi aur crawl karna shuru kar dega.

STEP 2 — Google My Business (SABSE ZAROORI 🌟)

Local business ke liye ye sabse bada ranking factor hai. "Solar Bikaner" search par yahi listing Map ke saath top par aati hai.

  1. google.com/business → Sign in → "Manage now".
  2. Business details bharo (EXACTLY website jaisa rakhna — naam, address, phone):

  3. Verification: Google postcard/SMS/call se verify karega (kuch din lagte hain).
  4. Installed projects ki photos upload karo (jitni zyada, utna achha).

STEP 3 — Reviews maango (ranking booster)

  • Har installation ke baad customer se Google review maango — WhatsApp par review link bhej do.
  • 4.5+ star rating + zyada reviews = Google ranking bahut upar.
  • Har review ka reply do (thank you message) — Google ko activity pasand hai.

STEP 4 — Free Directory Listings

In sab par free listing banao (NAP — Name, Address, Phone — sab jagah SAME rakhna):

  • JustDial
  • IndiaMART (parts business ke liye bahut achha)
  • Sulekha
  • Facebook Page + Instagram (business profile)

STEP 5 — Content (long-term)

Time-time par website/social par useful content daalo — Google fresh content ko reward karta hai:

  • Newsletter tab me regular updates (subsidy news, offers).
  • Installed projects ki nayi photos PhotoGroups me.
  • Naye customer reviews Testimonials me.
