🌞 Diamond Solar Website — Update Guide

Ye guide client ke liye hai. Website me naye products, videos, photos, testimonials aur news add karna sirf Google Sheet me likhne jitna easy hai. Koi coding nahi!

1️⃣ Ek Baar Ka Setup (sirf shuru me)

  1. Google Sheets me ek nayi spreadsheet banao (sheets.google.com).
  2. Neeche tabs banao (bottom me + dabakar), naam EXACTLY yahi rakho:
    Products | Projects | PhotoGroups | Testimonials | Newsletter | Settings
  3. Har tab ki pehli row me neeche diye column ke naam likho (chhote letters me).
  4. Sheet me upar right Share button → "Anyone with the link" → Viewer → Done.
  5. Sheet ke URL me se ID copy karo:
    docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/👉YE-LAMBA-CODE👈/edit
  6. Website folder me js/config.js file Notepad me kholo aur PASTE_YOUR_GOOGLE_SHEET_ID_HERE ki jagah wo ID paste karo. Save karo. Bas!

📹 Google Drive Se Video/Photo Ka Link Kaise Lein

  1. Drive me file upload karo.
  2. File par right-click → Share → "Anyone with the link" karo. (YE SABSE ZAROORI STEP HAI!)
  3. Copy link dabao aur Sheet me paste karo.
⚠️ Agar "Anyone with the link" nahi kiya to video/photo website par nahi dikhega (sirf aapko dikhega). Koi cheez na dikhe to pehle yahi check karo.
💡 Videos ke liye YouTube best hai (fast + unlimited views). Drive bhi chalega, lekin agar ek din me bahut log dekhein to Drive temporarily video block kar sakta hai.
